Afternoon arrival

🏨

Whenever you land

Check in & drop bags

Get settled, freshen up. The city is waiting — no rush.

🌿

Afternoon

Walk The High Line

NYC's elevated park built on old railway tracks. Start at Gansevoort St and walk north — great skyline views and street art along the way.

💡 Free entry · ~1.5 mi walk
🧺

Late afternoon

Chelsea Market

At the end of the High Line. Grab a snack or coffee inside this converted factory. Great energy, lots of food stalls.

Evening

🏘️

Before dinner

Wander West Village

The most charming streets in NYC — Bleecker, Perry, Charles. Cobblestones, brownstones, flowers. Great for a slow evening stroll.

🍝

Dinner

Dinner in the West Village

The neighborhood is full of great spots. Via Carota (Italian, no reservations — go early) or Buvette (French bistro, cozy & intimate).

🕖 Go before 6:30pm to avoid waits
🌃

After dinner

Night in Midtown

Swing by Times Square at night — touristy but undeniably cool after dark. Then grab a drink at any rooftop bar on the way back.

Morning

☕

Morning

Coffee & bagel

Grab a proper NYC bagel before heading out. Ess-a-Bagel or any local deli. You need fuel for the day.

🌳

Mid-morning

Central Park

Walk to Bow Bridge (the most photographed bridge in NYC) and Bethesda Fountain. In early April the cherry blossoms might still be out.

🌸 April = cherry blossoms season
🍪

On the way

Levain Bakery

Stop at Levain on 74th St (near Central Park). The cookies are legendary — thick, warm, worth every calorie. There's always a line but it moves fast.

Afternoon — Brooklyn

🌉

Early afternoon

Walk the Brooklyn Bridge

Walk from Manhattan → Brooklyn (about 30 min). Great views of the skyline from the top. Walk on the upper pedestrian deck.

📸 Halfway = best skyline photo
📸

After the bridge

Explore DUMBO

Head to Washington St for the iconic shot through the Manhattan Bridge arch. Jane's Carousel by the waterfront is beautiful too.

🍕

Lunch

Pizza in DUMBO

Grimaldi's or Juliana's — both legendary NYC pizza spots right under the bridge. Coal-fired, proper thin crust.

Evening

🌇

Sunset

Top of the Rock

Rockefeller Center observation deck — better views than Empire State because you can actually see the Empire State Building. Sunset timing is magic.

🎟️ Book tickets in advance online
🥂

Evening

Dinner & drinks

End the big day with a proper dinner. Midtown, Hell's Kitchen, or back downtown — lots of good options wherever you end up.

Morning send-off

🍳

10:30 AM · SoHo

Breakfast at Jack's Wife Freda

A SoHo classic. Mediterranean-inspired brunch, great coffee, beautiful space. The perfect last meal before heading home.

⭐ Reserved · 10:30 AM
🛍️

After breakfast

Stroll through SoHo

Great neighbourhood for a lazy post-brunch walk. Prince St and Spring St have cool shops, gallery windows, and street art.

✈️

When ready

Head home

That's a wrap. Best trip ever? Probably.
